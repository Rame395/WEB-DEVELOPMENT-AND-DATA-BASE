Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

🔐 1. Data Collection

In accordance with the Data Protection Act (DPA) and GDPR, this Hotel Management System collects only the minimum personal data required to provide booking services.

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address
  • Account Data: Login credentials (hashed)
  • Booking Data: Dates, room type, guests, price
  • Payment Status: Paid / Pending (no card storage)
🎯 2. Purpose of Processing

Your data is used strictly to:

  • Process hotel bookings
  • Send booking confirmations & receipts
  • Provide customer support

We do not sell, rent, or use your data for advertising or profiling.

⚖️ 3. Legal Basis for Processing
  • Contract: To fulfill hotel bookings
  • Consent: When you submit forms
  • Legal Obligation: For record keeping
🗑️ 4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account (Right to Erasure)
  • Restrict or object to processing

Deleting your account removes all associated bookings permanently.

⏳ 5. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as needed to fulfill bookings and legal obligations. Inactive accounts may be removed after a defined period.

🍪 6. Cookies & Sessions

We use session cookies to maintain login state and protect against CSRF attacks. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

🔗 7. Third-Party Services

We use secure third-party services for essential operations such as:

  • Email delivery (Flask-Mail)
  • Payment processing (if enabled)

All third parties comply with data protection regulations.

🛡️ 8. Security Measures
  • Password Hashing: PBKDF2 with salt
  • CSRF Protection: Token-based validation
  • Rate Limiting: Login & reset protection
  • RBAC: Admin vs User roles
📩 9. Contact

For any privacy-related concerns, contact us at: privacy@yourhotelapp.com
